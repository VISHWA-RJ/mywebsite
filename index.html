About Me

As a final-year B.Tech student, my objective is to transition from theoretical models to tangible, scalable AGI. My core philosophy rejects anthropomorphism in favor of pure system engineering. Intelligence, in my view, is an emergent property of globally coordinated, simple, distributed 'cells', each containing a core set of modules: Memory, Learning, Control, and Prediction.

My academic work at Arasu Engineering College has provided me with a robust understanding of hardware-software co-design, crucial for developing efficient implementations of these AGI cells. My ultimate goal is to establish a research center dedicated to this vision and to explore the technological frontier of mind uploading.
