About Me

I am a B.Tech student at Arasu Engineering College, driven by the goal of engineering true, domain-general artificial intelligence. My philosophy centers on creating AGI as a distributed system of intelligent 'cells', where global coordination gives rise to emergent intelligence, rather than mimicking human consciousness.

My academic background in Electronics and Communication Engineering provides a strong foundation in hardware, signal processing, and system design, which I apply to my primary research interest: building a functional distributed AGI and establishing a research center for mind uploading technologies.
